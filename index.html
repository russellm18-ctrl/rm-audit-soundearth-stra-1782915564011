// Findings

  • THE STRUCTURAL METADATA COLLAPSE: Your product images—Foaming Hand Soap, Cinnamon Essential Oil, Lemon Essential Oil—are almost certainly being served without descriptive alt-text, product-level JSON-LD schema, or embedded EXIF metadata tying them to any ingredient claim or use-case context. Search engines are reading them as blank files. You are bleeding the query "non-toxic foaming hand soap safe for pets" directly to competitors who have structured their product imagery as indexed data assets, not decorative placeholders.
  • THE HIDDEN TRUST DEFICIT: The eco-conscious household buyer—specifically the parent or pet owner who scans ingredient safety before purchasing cleaning products—lands on your Foaming Hand Soap or essential oil listings and finds zero visual proof of your "Safe for People, Pets and Planet" claim. No ingredient close-ups, no label transparency shots, no lifestyle context showing actual use in a home environment. A written tagline without visual substantiation is a liability, not a selling point, to a buyer trained to distrust greenwashing.
  • THE COMPETITIVE EXPOSURE: Direct competitors in the natural home care space—brands like Branch Basics or comparable DTC clean-cleaning lines—use clinical ingredient-disclosure photography and lifestyle sequencing that shows the product in actual domestic use contexts. Your Aroma Bracelet Gift Set at $29.49 and your essential oil range are priced competitively but photographed at commodity level, which collapses perceived value and kills the gifting and wellness-gifting conversion that should be your highest-margin transaction.
  • THE PHOTO-FIRST UPGRADE: Shoot every SKU in three mandatory asset types: isolated product on clean surface, ingredient or label close-up with legibility, and in-context domestic use. Every image file gets deployed with standardized alt-text referencing product name plus safety claim plus intended user ("pet-safe foaming hand soap for home use"), structured JSON-LD Product schema embedding price range, availability, and safety attributes, and EXIF copyright metadata locked before upload. This turns your existing product catalog into a crawlable, trust-building, conversion-structured data layer—not a static image gallery.
